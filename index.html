About Me

I am a master student of Graduate Institute of Biomedical Electronics and Bioinformatics (BEBI) at National Taiwan University (NTU). Currently, I am doing my bioinformatist internship at Cancer Science Institute of Singapore (CSI). Previously, I interned at i5k workspace @ NAL, USDA, Maryland, U.S.A and several companies (see details below or check my CV).

My research interests span over the fields of Bioinformatics, Cheminformatics and Machine Learning. In particular, I am interested in building pipeline to assist analysis of sequencing data of cancer patients. In my leisure time, I am a software developer of open-source projects. You can view my github profile or libraries.io profile to see my contributions.
